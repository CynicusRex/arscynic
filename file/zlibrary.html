How to access Z-Library?

Z-Library should be accessible via https://z-library.sk and https://go-to-library.sk. In case these domains don't work anymore, Z-Library's Wikipedia article usually has up-to-date URLs: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Z-Library

Regular browser

Can't seem to log in? Perhaps all one needs is a password reset 1 / password reset 2. Or there is a problem with the domain:

“Unfortunately, one of our primary login domains was seized today. Therefore, we recommend using the domain [singlelogin.re] to log in to your account, as well as to register. Please share this domain with others,”

“You can also restore access to your account or create a new one using our magic email address. Just send any letter from your mailbox to this email blackbox@zlib.se, and you will receive your personal access to Z-Library in the auto-generated letter within 3–5 minutes.” —Z-Library

Tor Browser

If the above method does not work, then access the Z-Library through Tor Browser.

Arch Linux: sudo pacman -S torbrowser-launcher. Any other Linux: itsfoss.com.

Use the following onion link in Tor Browser: zlibrary24tuxziyiyfr7zd46ytefdqbqd2axkmxm4o5374ptpc52fad.onion

—Angelino Desmet; 17 Jan 2023.
